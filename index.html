About

was born in Yokohama....

I was a community manager at Snapeee; Mindpalette Inc.

I love writing and taking a apicture and sometimes macha.

I reached the peak of my computer usage at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where I majored in visual communications within the journalism school. Thankfully, the carpal tunnel gods spared me and I graduated to join the Special Projects team at USA Today, where I designed magazines and earned the unofficial title of tech junkie. Then, a year ago I packed my bags and moved across the country to San Francisco to join Curio as a visual designer. My love for food and photography led me to OpenTable where I am a founding member of the new Experience Design team to re-imagine the OpenTable dining experience. Prior to this, I was fortunate enough to intern at fashion media capital, Condé Nast, where I was the creative director for the [n]tern magazine and ate lunch with Anna Wintour (only two tables away). On the rare occasion I am not on my computer, you'll find me updating my Instagram or trying out the newest foodie spot (see Tumblr—warning: lots of food photos).
