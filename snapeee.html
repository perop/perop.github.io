SNAPEEE

A photo sharing app 2013

Snapeee has over a million female users all over the world. The app is said it's a replacement of Instagram for a millennials especially in asian countries.

My role : UI Design, UX Design, Web/Mobile/Ads/Visual Design, Content
Design Process : Understanding, User research, Prototyping & usability testing
Design Tools : Sketch, Adobe XD, Invision
Timeline : 2 years

Rebranding

I was a 4th employee at Snapeee and helped the startup to lead a-million-users product with a growth team. First of all, I was responsible for creating our own persona and actively went out within the online community to connect with potential customers and advocate the brand.

As the service grew up, the company was facing a big change in branding and re-targeting its users and customers to monetize. I updated their user persona and colour palette for a more modern, adultlike feel and changed their typography to better suit their brand nature.

Rebranding

Product Details

After setting the goal for branding with the team, I speedly moved to advertising project and worked with sales team to provide an in-app-advertising to clients such as Fasion, media cosmetics companies to promote their products and brands.

With an amazing team, I was able to create advertisings in several screens on the app without not letting user feel uncomfortable and less-actively. I also created the ads system and its flow for a engineer team so that both of sales team and creative team can make sure we are on the same page for monetizing and geering up Snapeee app.
