Rebranding

I was a employee at Snapeee and helped the company lead a-million-users product with a growth team. First, I was responsible for creating our own persona and actively went out within the online community to connect with potential customers and advocate the brand.

As the service grew up, the company was facing a big change in branding and re-targeting its users and customer to monetize. I updated their user persona and colour palette for a more modern, adultlike feel & changed their typography to better suit their brand nature.
