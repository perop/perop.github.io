SO, HERE'S A LITTLE ABOUT ME...


For the past 6 years I've been working as an UX, UI and Product Designer for all things digital. I traveled 20 countries and learned the importance of diversity in perspective, and how healthy communities thrive in systems where value can be easily created and exchanged.

After my UX career at the successful advertising agency, Cyberagent Inc in Tokyo, I studied computer science and design at MakeSchool, which is a programming college backed by Y combinator, and decided to move to San Francisco to seek more adventures.
