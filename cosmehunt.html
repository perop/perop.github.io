iOS App and Website 2017

Cosme Hunt is the beauty platform where you to find the best cosmetics/beauty products within seconds.

My role: UI & UX Design
Design Process: Understanding, User research, Prototyping & usability testing
Design Tools: Sketch, Adobe XD, Invision
Timeline: 1 year

After digging deep into user needs and clearing a user flow and a comparision research, I derived the solution which is providing a mobile platform where everyone can communicate and talk about the beauty and get the products within the app. the app also allows users follow their soclal friends such as Facebook, Twitter and Instagram and let users browse what kind of products their friends are using and recommending by using spme API. The in-app purchase features I designed is also one of the keys of this app, as Cosme Hunt core users, millemials currently prefer online shopping, not going to drugstore.
